      <p>Brainrot is the humorous term for obsessive thinking, usually about fandoms, characters, or stories that won't leave your mind.</p>
    </section>

    <section id="symptoms">
      <h2>Symptoms of Brainrot</h2>
      <ul>
        <li>Constantly thinking about a fictional character</li>
        <li>Rewatching or rereading the same scenes endlessly</li>
        <li>Involuntarily quoting lines in your head (or aloud)</li>
        <li>Creating fan content at 3AM</li>
      </ul>
    </section>

    <section id="cope">
      <h2>How to Cope (or Not)</h2>
      <p>You could try distracting yourself, but let's be honest—you probably don’t want to. And that’s okay. Embrace it.</p>
